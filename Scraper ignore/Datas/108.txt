Develop and test the platform for new features to deliver an exceptional user experience for customers
Share your knowledge and zeel for user-centric design with the team
Collaborate closely with the CTO to deliver adequately on user and business requirements
Utilize Github/pull requests for source control and management

Job Requirements:

Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in Engineering, Computer Science (or equivalent experience)
At least 4+ years of relevant experience as a mobile engineer (Flutter)
Must be proficient with top front-end frameworks (React Native or Flutter)
Solid mobile experience and strong CSS skills
Nice to have some experience with Flutter/Dart and native iOS/Android development experience
Proficiency or willingness to learn Dart/Flutter and work on mobile applications
Be a team player with a collaborative, empathetic attitude to thrive with the ever-changing nature of a startup
