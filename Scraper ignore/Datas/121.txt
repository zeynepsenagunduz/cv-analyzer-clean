About the job
Job Description

This is a software development opportunity at one of the most successful Internet-based businesses in the world. With 62 million monthly visitors, more than 5 million listings, and over 15 billion monthly page views, we are in the top five largest classifieds platforms in the world and one of the top 300 largest Internet platforms globally. sahibinden.com is the first ever in Turkey to have been selected as one of top “20 best places to work in Europe” (2020).


What You Bring

As a sahibinden.com developer, generally speaking, you will be
developing new platform features within your business roadmap
working on technical features and participating in the technology transition projects
working to reduce overall bugs in your domain

We respect differences in personalities and varying approaches to getting your work done.
Having said that, we look for individuals who are

team players
resilient, result-oriented people
optimistic individuals
strong in analytical skills
Smart, analytical, and creative problem solver

An intricate code base, a large set of platform features and heavy performance numbers require individuals who can cope with the resulting software challenges. Technical qualifications we look for include:

4 year bachelor degree in CS or relevant field
Experience in the Java programming language
Minimum 2 year of software development experience in a hands on coding role with Java
Preferably experienced in data structures, algorithms, and performance analysis
Experienced with good software engineering practices, including the application of patterns and unit testing
Practical experience of SQL and NoSQL databases including data modelling
Core XP practices of TDD, Pair Programming, and Continuous Integration
Advocate for the best practices in software engineering


Bonus Points For:

Experience in the implementation of microservice architectures
Experience in building large-scale web applications
Containerization technologies such as Kubernetes and Docker
Experience with cloud technologies
