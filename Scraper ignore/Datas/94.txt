About the job
A rapidly-growing company building cutting-edge products that will enable businesses to improve hiring outcomes is looking for a React Front-End Engineer. The selected candidate will be responsible for defining and enhancing the organization’s product and technical roadmaps. The company's cutting-edge technology eliminates the guesswork in hiring by allowing businesses to discover and assess the abilities that matter. The company has managed to raise more than £125K in funding during their Pre-Seed round. This will be a full-time position.

Job Responsibilities:

Design the front-end architecture, deliver features, and ensure other team members follow the front-end’s best practices
Develop expertise on-the-job and make things happen
Have extreme attention to detail and concentrate on solving problems with the team

Job Requirements:

Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in Engineering, Computer Science (or equivalent experience)
At least 6+ years of relevant experience as a software developer
Must have experience working with TypeScript, ES5, 6, & 7
Prolific experience with RabbitMQ, TypeORM, PostgreSQL, and Jest
Extensive experience working with GitHub Action, AWS, Heroku, and microservices based on Node.js, NestJS, and TypeScript
At least 3 years of working with React
Solid expertise in Microservices interfacing, GraphQL, and Apollo Client
Must have a firm grasp of web fundamentals, DRY philosophy, and clean code
Must have prolific knowledge of design patterns and the basics of DevOps
Must be a problem solver, continuous learner, strive to adapt to change, and share knowledge 
Fluency in written and spoken English is necessary
