About the job
A U.S.-based company that is developing a cloud-based shipping platform that helps businesses save time and money on shipping, is looking for a Front-End Developer. The developer will be collaborating closely with the design team to create beautiful, interactive, and responsive interfaces. The company's automated shipping solutions provide businesses access to discounted rates from all major carriers, including USPS, FedEx, and UPS. The company has managed to securely raise more than $150mn during their Series E round of funding.

Job Responsibilities:

Develop new pages from beginning to end on your own or collaborate with other team members to lead the execution of the user interfaces
Working with design, identifying, proposing, and leading/assisting others in the development of reusable front-end components will help the application appear and feel more consistent
Streamline designs for implementation with early input from the design team, and take part in design evaluations for new features, products, and systems
Together with the design team, develop a tool for building interactive interfaces, rapid prototypes of design ideas, and designs that are responsive and accessible
Lead the construction of a UI testing framework and tests, architect the user interface layer to maximize design and code reuse, and deliver new pages and features
Develop the engineering team's UI competencies through mentoring, offer suggestions to make the product more usable, and simplify the user interface
Keep abreast of high-performance best practices, run performance tests frequently, continuously improve web pages, and promote the use of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript developments
