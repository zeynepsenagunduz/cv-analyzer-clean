 Experienced in process management or enterprise architecture from 2 to 4 years,

 Hands on experience on RPA tools such as UiPath, Blue Prism, Automation Anywhere, etc. UiPath experience is highly preferred,
 Being familiar with infrastructure and/or software development processes is a plus,
 Able to work independently as well as part of a team,

 Strong time and project management skills,

 Strong critical and quantitative thinking skills with high quality and detail orientation,
