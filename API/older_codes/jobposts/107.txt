About the job

Más de 400 empresas, incluídas Johnson & Johnson, Dell, Disney, Volvo, etc., han contratado a desarrolladores de Turing.

This will be a great chance for developers to work in a company making ground-breaking innovations.

Maintain the website built using React

Work through the complete software development lifecycle, from scratch to launch

Job Requirements:

Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in Engineering, Computer Science (or equivalent experience)
At least 3+ years of relevant experience as a software developer
4+ years of experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React
Interest in Blockchain and development experience is nice to have
Good English communication skills
