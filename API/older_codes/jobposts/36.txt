About the job
An online service that helps consumers make smarter shopping decisions is looking for a Front-end Engineer. The developer will be responsible for building exceptional mobile and desktop web applications. The company verifies reviews on popular platforms such as Amazon, Yelp, TripAdvisor, Apple App Store, etc. It has managed to raise $5.3 mn in Series A funding and is growing rapidly. This is an amazing opportunity for candidates to work with a software service that is used by millions of users. The candidate will be given a chance to move up the ladder while they showcase their skills. This is a full-time, long-term role and requires 6 hours overlap with EST.

Job Responsibilities:

Design and build efficient intuitive front-end applications 
Collaborate with Product Managers, Designers, and Software Engineers to deliver high-quality user-facing products
Interact with our customers’ technical leadership, understand their requirements, and execute the same
Work with a wide range of systems and technologies and solve problems from end-to-end

Job Requirements:

Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in Computer Science (or equivalent experience)
Previous experience as a Front-end engineer
Proficiency with React, Javascript, and CSS
Experience with React Native and Next.js is nice to have
Good communication skills and efficient team player
Can function effectively without too much hand-holding and micromanagement
