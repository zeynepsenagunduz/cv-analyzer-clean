About the job
We are looking for Enterprise Architects who will be part of our IT Architecture Team.


Responsibilities:

Document the architectural aspects of the application, data and technology layer and maintain the architecture framework;
Design & document architectural guidance and best practices
Develop conceptual, logical and physical data models to support data analysis and business intelligence
Define Core Problems in all applications and guide teams to solve problems related to Technical Debt, Performance, Architectural Design
Analyze & Design Integration Points between applications
POC processes & vendor management for IT products
Evaluate the architecture base and correct miss-alignments respecting the established architecture guidelines


Qualifications:

Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in computer engineering, information technology, or engineering
Minimum 5 years experience in Enterprise/IT Architecture domain,
Influence cross team initiatives and mentor junior engineers
Experience on analyzing, defining and documenting software architecture
Experience in working on data modelling tools (Erwin/Power Designer). Preferred Power Designer
Experience on Oracle, SQL and NoSQL databases
Knowledge on Java Technologies & Spring Framework
Good understanding of Microservice design philosophies (Autonomy, Event Sourcing...)
Knowledge in Event-Driven architecture
Understanding of SDLC and its execution requirements
Ability to consult IT Design & Architecture to whole IT Organization
Solid understanding of Software development best practices across SDLC
Familiarity with continuous integration/deployment processes and tools such as IDEs, Source Code management(GIT), Jenkins, Maven, Nexus artifacts, etc..
Participate Agile/Scrum methodologies
Willingness and passion to learn new methodologies, patterns and languages, solve complex problems
TOGAF certification is a plus
